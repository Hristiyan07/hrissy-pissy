Our Story

Founded in 2023, Hrissy-Pissy began with a simple mission: to create coffee that elevates the everyday ritual into an extraordinary experience.

Our beans are ethically sourced from small farms across the world's premier coffee regions, then meticulously roasted to bring out their unique character and complexity.

Each cup of Hrissy-Pissy coffee represents our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and the perfect brew.
